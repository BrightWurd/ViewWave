Tech Giants Face New Regulations in Europe
The European Union has introduced new regulations aimed at curbing the power of major technology companies. The Digital Markets Act (DMA) and Digital Services Act (DSA) will impose stricter rules on data sharing, user privacy, and market dominance. Companies like Google, Apple, and Facebook will be significantly affected by these changes.
Global Markets React to Fed Rate Hike
Global financial markets have reacted to the Federal Reserve's decision to raise interest rates by 0.25%. The move is seen as a measure to combat rising inflation, but it has also led to volatility in stock markets worldwide. Analysts are divided on the long-term impact of this decision.
Climate Summit 2024: Key Agreements Reached
World leaders have reached several key agreements at the 2024 Climate Summit in Nairobi. These include commitments to reduce carbon emissions by 45% by 2030 and to increase funding for renewable energy projects in developing countries. The summit marks a significant step forward in global efforts to combat climate change.
